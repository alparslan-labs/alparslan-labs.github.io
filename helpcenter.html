Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer trading services?
No. We are not an exchange and do not execute any trades. Our focus is on delivering scientific solutions for investment analytics and actionable insights countering the asymmetries in financial markets. We empower investors and organizations with tools to make informed decisions but leave the trading and custody to you.
Are you a robo-advisor?
No, we are a research lab. When using our services you own and remain in full control of your assets at all times. We will never require you for share your account details.
How can your analytics help me as an investor?
Our platform equips you with cutting-edge tools for asset selection, portfolio optimization, and strategy development. By leveraging our advanced analytics, you can uncover hidden opportunities, minimize risk, and gain a competitive edge.
Do I need a technical background to use your platform?
Not at all. While our tools are powered by sophisticated algorithms, the user interface is intuitive and designed for investors of all backgrounds. Whether you're a seasoned professional or new to analytics, you'll find value in our solutions.
Can your analytics integrate with my existing workflows?
Yes! Our platform is designed to seamlessly integrate into your existing investment or business workflows, ensuring minimal disruption while enhancing decision-making processes.
Is your platform suitable for retail investors or institutional clients?
Both! Our analytics are flexible enough to cater to retail investors seeking clarity and institutional clients aiming to scale and refine their strategies with latest research findings.
How often do you update your analytics models?
We continuously refine and update our models to incorporate the latest market data and research advancements. Staying ahead of the curve is our top priority.
I do not understand anything. How can I learn?
We will soon provide a getting started section and investment fundamentals courses for retail investors. Enterprise customers should be familiar with most of the concepts discussed.
Can I get a trial or demo of your platform?
Yes, we offer limited access to explore our features. Join our waitlist or reach out to us to learn more about how our analytics can benefit you.
I got scammed before. Do you offer asset insurance?
As we are not offering any custody services, we have zero control over your assets. Thus, the answer is trivial: we do not insure what we do not hold.
Can individuals access enterprise solutions?
Due to the technical complexity and costs of features involved, we limit the access to your advantage.
