The Scientific Background of Akinji Labs

But, what is Akinji Labs? The clue is in the name. we are a research lab, of course. In a nutshell, we explore the frontiers of information technology research to build algorithmic investment solutions. We apply our Math, Data and Computer Science to design and improve systematic quantitative methodologies for risk and asset management purposes.

Most of us are fully devoted to Research & Development. Our teams has different backgrounds, but they are all mad about finding state-of-the-art solutions to challenges in finance. This includes exploring every technique, every unanswered question and every path that may lead us to our goals.

Each of our products is one of those paths. A space to experiment, to learn and to seek for creativity. A place to share, to keep learning. Above all, a space to dare, experiment, learn and earn from trial and error.
